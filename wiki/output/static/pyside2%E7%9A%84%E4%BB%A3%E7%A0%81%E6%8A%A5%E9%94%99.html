pyside2的代码报错

2020年11月25日 11:00
pyqt
  • 运行pyside2的代码报错
qt.qpa.plugin: Could not load the Qt platform plugin "windows" in "" even though it was found.
This application failed to start because no Qt platform plugin could be initialized. Reinstalling the application may fix this problem.

Available platform plugins are: direct2d, minimal, offscreen, windows.

https://www.cnblogs.com/hankleo/p/13432119.html

  • 解决办法
    C:\Anaconda2\envs\qt\Lib\site-packages\PySide2\plugins\platforms
    替换
    C:\Anaconda2\envs\qt\Library\plugins\platforms
  • 复制之后可以正常运行，但是界面与qt designer预览的不一致
  • 彻底的解决办法是，安装库的时候，已经安装了pyside2就不要再安装pyqt
  • 参看commandcenter python2迁移到python3
